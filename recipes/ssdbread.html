Simple Sourdough Bread

Description

Simple 3-ingredients sourdough bread anyone can make at their home.

Follow carefully through the recipe and bake your own first sourdough loaf:

Ingredients:

  • Wheat flower (500 gr.)
  • Water (300 ml)
  • Salt (12 gr.)
  • Sourdough starter* (200 gr.)

*If you don't have a starter already, simply follow this link to make yourself one!

Steps:

  1. Mixing the ingredients:
    1. Take a large pot and poor in 300 ml luke warm water.
    2. Add 200 gr. of your sourdough starter and stir to mix.
    3. Add 12 gr. of salt and mix together.
    4. Add 500 gr. of White bread flour and mix together.

  2. Working the dough:
    1. Carefully mix all the ingredients so there is no dry flour patches left
    2. Kneed your dough until the gluten network is developed.
    3. If you are not sure if your gluten network is develpped enpugh you can perform window blind test.
    4. Shape your dough into a bowl and let it rest for 3 hours on room temperature.
    5. Shape your dough once more and put it in clean bowl for final fermentation.
    6. Now you have two options depending on you schedule:
      • Leave the dough to ferment on room temperature for 3 hours, or
      • Put it in refrigerator overnight and bake in the morning.

  3. Baking the bread:
    1. Preheat the owen to 230 degrees Celsius
    2. Transfer your dough into baking pot with the lid on
    3. Bake for 50 minutes:
      • bake covered for 30 minutes adn then
      • uncover and continue baking for another 20 minutes
    4. Cover with clean cloth ann let it rest for at least 3 hours.
